WHAT IS THE CABRILLO?

Local to San Luis Obispo and apart of the Cal Poly community, the Cabrillo is a place that provides the Cal Poly golf team and their supporters with a place to play the game while building personal connections with one another. While there are other courses in the area such as Dairy Creek and Laguna Lake, the Cabrillo is special in the way that it is short yet the most challenging compared to its competitors. The Cabrillo is a place every player and team wants to visit and play when in California.
